## 1. Facebook Ad Copy Generator

**From: Marketing & Advertising Pack**

Create high-converting Facebook ads using the proven Hook-Story-Offer framework.

```

Create 3 variations of ad copy using the Hook-Story-Offer framework:
1. Pattern Interrupt Hook (first 1-2 lines that stop the scroll)
2. Relatable Story (builds connection and credibility)
3. Irresistible Offer (clear CTA with urgency element)

For each variation, use a different emotional angle:
- Variation A: Fear of missing out / exclusivity
- Variation B: Transformation / aspiration
- Variation C: Social proof / authority

Format each ad with:
- Primary text (125 characters or less for preview)
- Full ad body
- Suggested headline (40 characters)
- Description (30 characters)
- CTA button recommendation
```

**How to use:** Fill in the bracketed fields with your specific product details. Test all 3 variations—you can't predict which angle will win!

## 2. SEO Blog Post Generator

**From: Content Writing Pack**

Create comprehensive blog posts optimized for search engines and readers.

```
You are an expert content strategist and SEO specialist with 15+ years of experience creating content that ranks on page 1 of Google.

I need you to write a comprehensive blog post on the following topic:
**Topic:** [YOUR TOPIC]
**Target keyword:** [PRIMARY KEYWORD]
**Secondary keywords:** [2-3 RELATED KEYWORDS]
**Target audience:** [WHO IS THIS FOR]
**Desired word count:** [1500-3000 WORDS]

Follow this process:

**Step 1: Search Intent Analysis**
First, analyze what someone searching for this keyword actually wants to learn. Consider:
- What questions are they trying to answer?
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- What stage of awareness are they at?

**Step 2: Create the Content Structure**
Design an outline that:
- Hooks readers in the introduction with a compelling statistic, story, or provocative statement
- Uses H2 and H3 headers that naturally incorporate keywords
- Includes at least one "featured snippet opportunity" section
- Flows logically and keeps readers engaged

**Step 3: Write the Full Article**
Create the complete blog post with:
- An engaging introduction (hook + context + thesis)
- Comprehensive body sections with actionable insights
- Real examples, data points, or case studies
- A conclusion with clear next steps

**Step 4: Optimize for SEO**
At the end, provide:
- Meta title (under 60 characters)
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- 5 suggested internal linking opportunities
```

**How to use:** Research your keyword's search volume before writing. Provide specific audience details for better results.

## 3. Expert Code Review

**From: Coding & Development Pack**

Get senior-engineer-level feedback on your code with actionable improvements.

```
You are a senior software engineer with 15+ years of experience. You've contributed to major open-source projects and have a reputation for thorough, constructive code reviews.

Review the following code:

[PASTE YOUR CODE HERE]

**Context:**
- Language/Framework: [LANGUAGE/FRAMEWORK]
- Purpose of this code: [WHAT IT DOES]
- Environment: [PRODUCTION/STAGING/DEVELOPMENT]

Provide a comprehensive review covering:

**1. CRITICAL ISSUES (Must Fix)**
- Location, issue description, risk level, specific fix with code

**2. CODE QUALITY (Should Fix)**
- Readability, naming conventions, organization, DRY/SOLID violations

**3. PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS**
- Time complexity with explanation
- Space complexity with explanation
- Optimization suggestions with code

**4. SECURITY REVIEW**
- Potential vulnerabilities
- Input validation assessment

**5. REFACTORED VERSION**
Provide the improved code with comments explaining changes.
```

**How to use:** Include relevant context about your code's purpose. The more specific, the better the review.

## 4. Strategic SWOT Analysis

**From: Business & Strategy Pack**

Get McKinsey-level strategic analysis for any business decision.

```
You are a McKinsey-trained strategy consultant with 20 years of experience advising Fortune 500 companies.

Conduct a comprehensive SWOT analysis for:

**Business/Initiative:** [COMPANY OR PROJECT NAME]
**Industry:** [SECTOR]
**Context:** [CURRENT SITUATION]
**Time horizon:** [NEXT 6 MONTHS / 1 YEAR / 3 YEARS]

Provide strategic SWOT analysis:

**1. STRENGTHS** (Internal Positive)
| Strength | Evidence | Sustainability | Strategic Leverage |

**2. WEAKNESSES** (Internal Negative)
| Weakness | Impact | Fixability | Priority |

**3. OPPORTUNITIES** (External Positive)
| Opportunity | Size | Timing | Fit |

**4. THREATS** (External Negative)
| Threat | Probability | Impact | Timeframe |

**5. STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS**
- SO Strategies (Strengths to capture Opportunities)
- WO Strategies (Overcome Weaknesses for Opportunities)
- ST Strategies (Strengths to avoid Threats)
- WT Strategies (Minimize Weaknesses and avoid Threats)

**6. PRIORITIZED ACTION PLAN**
Top 5 actions with timeline, owner, and expected impact.
```

**How to use:** Be brutally honest about weaknesses—that's where the real value is.

## 5. Weekly Planning System

**From: Productivity Pack**

Transform how you plan your weeks with this structured approach.

```
You are a productivity coach who helps professionals achieve peak performance.

Help me plan my week:

**Calendar constraints:** [MEETINGS, DEADLINES]
**Top priorities:** [MUST GET DONE]
**Ongoing projects:** [IN PROGRESS]
**Hours available for deep work:** [ESTIMATE]

Provide:

**WEEK THEME**
One sentence capturing what success looks like this week.

**BIG 3 OUTCOMES**
The three things that would make this week a success.

**DAILY BREAKDOWN**
For each day, provide:
- Morning focus block (most important work)
- Midday tasks
- Afternoon wrap-up
- Time buffers for unexpected items

**NOT DOING LIST**
Tasks explicitly deprioritized this week and why.

**OBSTACLE PREVENTION**
Top 3 things likely to derail the plan and how to prevent each.

**WEEKLY METRICS**
How I'll know if this was a successful week.
```

**How to use:** Complete this on Sunday evening or Friday afternoon for maximum effectiveness.
